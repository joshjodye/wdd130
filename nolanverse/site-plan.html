Nolanverse Site Plan

Joshua Figueroa Cabrera

WDD 130-1

Overview

Purpose

I want to create a site that showcases the extraordinary work of filmmaker Sir Christopher Nolan. His movies have made a big impact on my life and I want to create something that will assist others in learning about his work by introducing them to the movies, the cast, a non-spoiler plot explanation, and an authors note as to why that movie has made the impact it has on my life.

Audience

This will draw people who are into filmmaking and are wanting to discover the filmography of well-known directors. Also people who are looking to get into Chris Nolan's movies, or rather movies in general.
