El Salvador

May 27th, 1912

Known for its scenic volcanoes, beaches, and rich cultural history, El Salvador has a growing economy largely based on remittances and services. Their flag consists of three horizontal stripes: blue on the top and bottom and white in the center. The blue represents the sky and the Pacific Ocean, while the white symbolizes peace and unity.
